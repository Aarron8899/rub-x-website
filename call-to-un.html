Urgent Call to the United Nations

Why the World Cannot Wait Any Longer

The Rub-X system offers a real-world, cost-effective, and energy-efficient solution to automate rubber tapping in rugged terrains – a crucial, often neglected sector.

Despite successful demonstrations, verified proof of function, and repeated outreach to international institutions, Rub-X has not received the pilot support or hosting needed to scale.

“Rub-X is not a concept. It is a working system. We now urge the UN and relevant global institutions to take action — not just acknowledge.”
