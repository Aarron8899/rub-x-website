Why Fairness Matters

Independent inventors are too often sidelined in the global response to real-world challenges. The Rub-X project stands as a proven, practical, and ready-to-deploy solution for sustainable agriculture, yet it has faced years of neglect from institutional channels that claim to support innovation.

Our Demands

  • Recognition of field-proven independent technologies
  • Inclusion in UN-backed trials, funding, and scaling programs
  • Accountability for institutions that delay or block innovation access

Rub-X: A Case for Action

Rub-X is a cost-effective, durable, and energy-efficient automation system for rubber tapping. It has been built, tested, and verified — yet remains unsupported by those tasked with advancing sustainability. We challenge UN agencies and global stakeholders to act not just in words, but in real pilot support.
