Our Mission

Rub-X is an innovative solution designed to automate rubber harvesting in hilly plantation regions. By leveraging a low-cost, reliable cable-driven system, we break through the long-standing bottlenecks of power and control distribution across thousands of trees.

Why Rub-X?

  • 🚜 Engineered for steep, rural rubber-growing terrains
  • 🔧 Reliable cable-drive system – no complex electronics needed
  • 🌱 Enables smallholder farmers to benefit from automation
  • 🌍 Aligned with climate-resilient and sustainable development goals

Partnership & Pilot Deployment

We are currently seeking institutional partners for pilot deployment, funding, and international support. Our registered trademark and US-based entity (Hanmin LLC) allow us to collaborate globally with UN agencies, NGOs, and investors.
