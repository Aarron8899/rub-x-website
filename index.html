🔶 Our Mission

Rub-X offers a breakthrough solution to the world’s toughest rubber harvesting challenge: How do you bring automation to remote, steep plantations — affordably, reliably, and at scale? We answered it not with robots or AI platforms, but with something simpler — and more powerful. Rub-X uses a low-cost cable-driven architecture to bring programmable automation across thousands of trees, even in the roughest terrains. We are not following the smart agriculture trend — we are how it becomes real.

🔶 Why Rub-X?

  • 🚜 Designed for steep, underdeveloped rubber-growing regions
  • 🔧 No robotics, no complex sensors — low-cost, low-maintenance
  • 🌱 Empowers smallholders with practical, affordable automation
  • 🌍 Built for sustainability and global climate goals
  • 🧩 Installs into existing plantations — no redesign required

🔶 Product Overview

Rub-X revolutionizes natural rubber harvesting with two globally unique innovations:

  • 🪢 Cable-driven automation – A rugged steel cable system that delivers programmable tapping with ultra-low energy use, across hills, slopes, and dense plantations.
  • 🧪 Latex pipeline collection system – A passive-flow infrastructure that reduces manual labor, ensures latex quality, and simplifies collection logistics.
  • ✅ No robots. No cloud. No nonsense. Rub-X is engineered for the real-world — not the lab.
