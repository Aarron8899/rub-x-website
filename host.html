Rub-X Institutional Hosting Cooperation

Important: Rub-X will no longer be promoted or licensed commercially. Institutional hosting is now the sole acceptable mode of cooperation. Without a trusted hosting institution, this technology will remain archived.

What Is Institutional Hosting?

Rub-X is a field-ready, low-cost, terrain-adaptable automation system designed to replace hazardous manual rubber tapping. We are seeking one or more responsible institutions to formally host this technology for verification, field trials, and coordinated deployment.

Hosting does not require purchasing or owning the patent. It entails taking public responsibility for managing, testing, and guiding Rub-X through institutional channels, in line with global sustainability goals.
