Rub-X: Three Years of International Response Record

This page documents the efforts, responses, and institutional feedback (or lack thereof) received since the introduction of the Rub-X autonomous tapping system – a field-verified innovation for the rubber industry.

Background

Rub-X is the world's first low-cost, autonomous tapping and latex collection solution, specifically designed for hilly, remote rubber plantations. It overcomes longstanding challenges in labor shortage, precision cutting, and terrain adaptability.

The system is now proven to be technically feasible, with working prototypes, video demonstrations, and interest from multiple countries and investors.

Milestones and Validation Efforts (2021–2024)

  • Functional prototype completed and tested in field conditions
  • Expert review facilitated by Confexhub (Malaysia): feasibility confirmed
  • Presented at the International Rubber Research Forum (IRRF) in Kuala Lumpur
  • Initial investor interest and trial site proposals in ASEAN countries
  • Working demonstration video published publicly (see below)
