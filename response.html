Rub-X: Ten-Year Development Records（2015–2025）

Through a comprehensive understanding of Rub-X’s ten-year journey—including its technical evolution, functional prototype demonstrations, and animation presentations—it becomes clear that this is not an unrealistic concept, but the result of years of dedicated development. The system has already gained recognition from experts and industry professionals across multiple countries.

Yet the core challenges hindering the sustainable development of the global natural rubber industry remain unresolved. Despite being the only known viable solution to date, Rub-X has received no meaningful support over the years—instead facing persistent obstacles and neglect. This reality is both disheartening and deeply troubling.
