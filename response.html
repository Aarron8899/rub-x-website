Background

Rub-X is the world's first low-cost, autonomous tapping and latex collection solution, specifically designed for hilly, remote rubber plantations. It overcomes longstanding challenges in labor shortage, precision cutting, and terrain adaptability.

The system is now proven to be technically feasible, with working prototypes, video demonstrations, and interest from multiple countries and investors.

Current Partnership Focus: Hosting & Field Testing Support

We are currently inviting international organizations and institutions to join us in hosting and field-validating Rub-X under real-world plantation conditions.

We are seeking partners that can:

  • Host or support field-testing sites for Rub-X systems
  • Contribute to validation, refinement, or documentation of results
  • Participate in knowledge transfer and system deployment for broader use

If your institution is interested in participating or offering support, please contact us. We welcome collaborative proposals aligned with sustainability, automation, and the future of fair rural livelihoods.
